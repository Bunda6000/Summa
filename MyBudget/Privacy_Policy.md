# Privacy Policy — Summa: Budget Planner

**Developer:** Steady Intelligence  
**Last updated:** May 9, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Thank you for using **Summa**. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, your rights regarding that information, and how to delete your data.

By creating an account and using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Account Data
To use Summa you create an account with your **email address** and a password. Your email is used to authenticate you, send verification emails, and allow you to recover your account. We do not share your email with any third party.

### Financial Data You Enter
All financial data — including income, expenses, budgets, and categories — is entered by you. This data is stored securely in our cloud database (Supabase, hosted in the EU) so that it syncs across your devices and is not lost if you reinstall the app.

### Subscription & Billing Data
If you purchase a paid plan, Google Play processes the payment on our behalf. We receive a purchase token, product ID, and renewal date from Google Play to confirm and maintain your subscription status. We never receive or store your payment card details.

### Device & Usage Data
The app may collect basic diagnostic information such as crash reports to help us identify and fix technical issues. This data does not contain personal or financial information.

---

## 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- **No location data.** The app does not request or use your location.
- **No financial account access.** The app does not connect to your bank or any financial institution.
- **No advertising identifiers.** We do not track you across apps for advertising purposes.

---

## 4. How We Use the Information

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Email address | Account authentication, verification, and password recovery |
| Financial data | Sync your budget across devices |
| Subscription data | Granting and managing access to paid features |
| Diagnostic data | Fixing bugs and improving app stability |

We do not use any data for advertising, profiling, or third-party marketing.

---

## 5. Data Storage & Security

- **Account and financial data** is stored on Supabase infrastructure with row-level security policies that ensure you can only access your own data.
- **Data in transit** is protected by HTTPS/TLS at all times.
- **Passwords** are never stored in plaintext; authentication is handled by Supabase Auth which uses bcrypt hashing.
- **Sensitive session tokens** are stored using secure on-device storage mechanisms (Android Keystore via Capacitor Preferences).
- No passwords, tokens, or personal data are written to application logs.

---

## 6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. The only data sharing that occurs is:

- **Supabase** (database and auth provider) — processes your account and financial data under a data processing agreement.
- **Google Play** (payment processor) — processes subscription payments. Their privacy policy governs payment data.

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

- **Access** the data we hold about you.
- **Correct** inaccurate data.
- **Delete** your data (see Section 8 below).
- **Restrict** or **object to** processing.
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at filip.bundovski@gmail.com.

## 8. Account & Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your account at any time from the **Account** screen inside the app. Upon deletion:

- Your account credentials are removed from our authentication system.
- All financial data, profile information, subscription records, and purchase history associated with your account are permanently deleted from our database.
- This process is immediate and irreversible.

If you cannot access the app, email us at filip.bundovski@gmail.com and we will delete your data within 30 days.
