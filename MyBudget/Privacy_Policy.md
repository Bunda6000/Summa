# Privacy Policy — Expenses & Budget Tracker

**Developer:** Steady Intelligence  
**Last updated:** April 26, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Thank you for using **Expenses & Budget Tracker**. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Financial Data You Enter
All financial data — including income, expenses, budgets, and categories — is entered by you and stored **locally on your device**. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to any external server.

### Device & Usage Data
The app may collect basic diagnostic information such as crash reports or performance data to help us identify and fix technical issues. This data does not contain any personally identifiable information and is used solely to improve app stability.

---

## 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- **No account or registration required.** You can use the app fully without providing any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
- **No location data.** The app does not request or collect your location.
- **No financial account access.** The app does not connect to your bank, payment provider, or any financial institution.

---

## 4. How We Use the Information

The limited diagnostic data collected is used exclusively to:

- Identify and resolve technical bugs and crashes
- Improve the overall performance and stability of the app

We do not use any data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

---

## 5. Sharing of Information

**We do not share any data with third parties.** No personal data, financial data, or device data is sold, rented, or disclosed to any external company, advertiser, or analytics provider.

---

## 6. Data Storage & Security

All financial data you enter into the app is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server. You are in full control of your data. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is deleted.

We take reasonable technical measures to protect any diagnostic data that may be collected during app operation.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it promptly.

---

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
